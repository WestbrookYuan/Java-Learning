System Scale
---

* All numbers in the basis of the computer is binary
* Usually use:
  * Binary:0-1
    * start with **0b** or **0B**
  * Decimal:0-9
  * Octal:0-7
    * start with **0**
  * Hex:0-9 and A-F
    * start with **0X** or **0x**
